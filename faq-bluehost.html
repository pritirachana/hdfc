What is Bluehost Maestro?

The Maestro program is Bluehost’s initiative to serve Web Professionals (WordPress website creators, designers and developers) with the right tools to take care of all your website management and client servicing needs. In this early version of Maestro, you get an account that lets you organize all your WordPress sites, categorize them by clients, manage clients’ hosting account, buy hosting securely on behalf of your client, invoicing services and much more. For each site you add, you will get one-click access to the WordPress Admin and Bluehost hosting account. As your clients grow - Maestro will help remove your overhead of managing multiple accounts for each client, by bringing it all together on a single dashboard. Find out more at https://maestro.bluehost.com/ .
