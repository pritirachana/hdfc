Offers

Do I get special discount on property booking?

Yes, you must have received a cashback voucher.

How can I redeem the voucher?

You can give your coupon code to the developer at the time of property booking. The developer will then verify your coupon code with GIHED CREDAI and give you the benefits.

Which developers are giving the cashback offer?

All participating developers of GIHED CREDAI Property Show 2020 will be giving you the cashback offer.

Till when can I use the cashback voucher?

The cashback voucher can be redeemed until 31st December 2020

Are there any terms and conditions applicable for the voucher?

Yes, there are certain terms and conditions applicable to redeem the cashback vouchers. These are mentioned below.

  • Valid only for property purchase and not on purchase of loans, allied industry products or other items
  • Redeemable only on properties of developers participating in GIHED CREDAI Virtual Property Show 2020. Valid till 31st December 2020
  • Only 1 voucher can be redeemed on purchase
  • Voucher is transferrable to your family member but cannot be clubbed with any other offer of developer.
  • One voucher can be used only once
  • Cash redemption criteria
    • Property Cost - Cash redemption
    • upto 50 lacs - 50000/-
    • Above 51 lacs - 100000/-
  • All disputes & conflicts subject to Ahmedabad jurisdiction only

THow do I get project details of all exhibitors?

When you access the ‘What is Where’ booklet, you can find all developers’ project details within it. Click here to download the booklet

How can I visit a specific stall?

There are multiple ways to visit any stall.

  • You can click on the company name or stall number in the ‘What is Where’ window to visit the stall
  • You can click on the company logos scrolling at the bottom in the lobby
  • You can click on the company logo on the logo panels visible in the lobby

How can I find the property of my choice?

You can use the ‘What is Where’ feature to filter and shortlist the prospective properties. Further, you can visit the stalls and interact with the developers directly for further details.

How can I get more information about any property?

You can interact with the developer at any stall through the chat feature. You can also view brochures, video ads and branding panels from each stall.

How can I get in touch with the representatives at the stall?

Each stall has a chat feature. When you click on ‘Chat’, your text interaction with the representative will start. In case, they are not occupied or not available, you can schedule a call back.

Can I schedule meeting with any particular exhibitor?

Yes. You can visit the developers’ stall and click on the Chat option in order to schedule a meeting with a representative of the exhibitor.

 What should I do if I cannot reach sales representative of the particular stall?

TThe sales representative might be busy at that moment. You can request for a call back from the respective stall.

Is there a video chat feature available?

TYes, this feature is available for all sponsors. You can request the sponsors for a video chat through Chat button from their stalls.

Can I book any property online?

You can surely connect with the respective developer and book the property. However, please note that GIHED CREDAI does not have any role in the sales/purchase of any property.
