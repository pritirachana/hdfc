SPEAKER PROFILE #VIRTEX2020

A K Jain

Managing Director, Rajasthan Electronics & Instruments Ltd, India

Mr A K Jain has worked for Rajasthan Electronics and Instruments Limited (REIL) for 36 years in various capacities and disciplines. His leadership and out-of-box approach resulted in high growth for REIL and the launch of several innovative solutions - World’s first Electronic Milk-Adulteration Tester, Largest 11 MW off-grid Solar Project at 9400 Gram Panchayats, 1 MWp Solar Power Project at Katra Railway Station, over 1,00,000 solar systems for low cost rural housing in Uttar Pradesh, Rajasthan and Tamil Nadu. REIL emerged as the largest off-grid solution provider and the largest deployer of milk analyser in India, benefiting sixty million citizens and three hundred thousand villages.He has been a key professional in Government’s Missions like National Dairy Plan, Solar Mission, Make in India, Digital India, National Electric Mobility Mission and Swachh Bharat.

A K Vishwanath Sharma

Deputy General Manager & Head, Plant Engineering, Toyota Kirloskar Motor, India

Mr Sharma joined Toyota Kirloskar Motor (TKM) in 1997 as Engineer during the plant construction, and has since served in various capacities.
He is presently working as DGM in Plant Adminstration Division, and is responsible for day-to-day plant administrative operations to ensure Safety & Environmental compliance at TKM plants and adherence to safety standards as per Toyota guidelines while complying with all environmental and legal standards; driving the TKM Jiritsuka (self-reliance) activities across the plant (Safety, Cost, Environment, Standardised work), and acting as single window for manufacturing to other functions.
As a part of Plant Engg and Engg Department he has been responsible for operation & maintenance of utilities; planning and execution of plant-wide expansions (Civil, Mech, Elect); setting up common maintenance for all shops (Press, Weld, Paint, Assembly, Utility), and plant-wide energy reduction activities.
Mr Sharma completed his BE from Bangalore University in 1993.

A K Jain

Director General, The Energy & Resources Institute, India

Dr Ajay Mathur is the Director General of TERI - The Energy & Resources Institute, and a member of the Prime Ministerial Council on Climate Change. Previously, he was the Director General of the Bureau of Energy Efficiency in the Government of India from 2006 to 2016, responsible for bringing energy efficiency into our homes, offices, and factories, through initiatives such as the star labelling programme for appliances, the Energy Conservation Building Code, and the Perform, Achieve and Trade programme for energy-intensive industries. Dr Mathur was earlier with TERI from 1986 to 2000, and then headed the Climate Change Team of World Bank in Washington DC. He was President of Suzlon Energy Limited, also headed the interim Secretariat of the Green Climate Fund. He has been a key Indian climate-change negotiator, and was also the Indian spokesperson at the 2015 climate negotiations at Paris.

Angus McCrone

Chief Editor, Bloomberg New Energy Finance, United Kingdom

Mr Angus McCrone has overall editing responsibility for BNEF’s research on clean energy and transport, working closely with BNEF’s 120-strong team of analysts. He writes about energy investment trends, project finance, and early-stage technologies. Before joining New Energy Finance in 2007, Mr McCrone was a financial journalist for UK national newspapers for 18 years. He also worked as senior economist for a leading London-based think tank, and founded an FSA-authorised financial services firm. Mr McCrone has a MSc Economic History, London School of Economics, MA Economics and Modern History, St Andrews University and Certificate of Investment Management

SPEAKER PROFILE #VIRTEX2020

Anil Sharma

Minister, MPP, Power & Non Conventional Energy Sources, Government of Himachal Pradesh, India

Anil Sharma was first elected to the State Legislative Assembly in November 1993. He served as a Rajya Sabha Member from 1998 to 2004.
He was Rural Development and Panchayati Raj Minister of the State of Himachal Pradesh from 2013 to 2017. He has been re-elected to 13th Vidhan Sabha of the State of Himachal Pradesh in December 2017 and inducted into the Council of Ministers as Multi Purpose Projects, Power and Non Conventional Energy Sources Minister.
As Minister of Multi Purpose Projects, Power and Non Conventional Energy Sources Minister, he has been instrumental in introducing policy initiatives in promotion of Solar and other renewable sources of energy in the State.

Arundhati Bhattacharya

Former Chairperson, State Bank of India, India

Mrs Arundhati Bhattacharya was first ever Women Chairperson of State Bank of India, a Fortune 500 company and served as the Chairperson till she retired in October 2017 She has expertise & deep understanding and insights of Financial services, particularly of Banking industry and related areas of Digital Banking. She was awarded Forbes Magazine ranked her 25th in their List of Most Powerful Women in the world and 5th in Most Powerful Women in Finance and was also included in the Top 100 Global Thinkers in 2014 by Foreign Policy Magazine. Mrs Bhattacharya was featured in Fortune List of Top 50 Globally Most Powerful Women in business and Ranked among Top 5 in the Asia Pacific region. She is associated with various institutions and initiatives for empowering the challenged and differently able, with an aim of integrating them in society.
